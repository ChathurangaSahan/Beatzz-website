Billie Eilish

With her unique sound and introspective lyrics, Billie Eilish has become one of the most popular pop artists in recent years.Her debut album "When We All Fall Asleep won multiple Grammys.


Best songs: Bad Guy, Everything I Wanted, When The Party's Over...

Taylor Swift

With her storytelling lyrics and catchy melodies, Taylor Swift has become a pop music icon.


Best songs: Love Story, Paper Rings, Blank Space...

Harry Styles

After leaving the boy band One Direction, Harry Styles has become a successful solo artist.His music blends pop, rock, and folk influences and has earned him critical acclaim.


Best songs: As It Was, Watermelon Sugar,Adore You...

Dua Lipa

Dua Lipa has become one of the most successful pop artists in recent years. Her sophomore album "Future Nostalgia" won the Grammy award for Best Pop Vocal Album.


Best songs: New Rules, Levitating, Don't Start Now...

Ariana Grande

With her powerful vocals and catchy pop songs, Ariana Grande has become a pop music icon. Her most recent album "Positions" showcases her versatility as an artist.


Best songs: Positions, One Last time, 7 Rings...
