GRAMMYs

Introduction

Grammy Awards is presented by the Recording Academy of the United states of America. This year, it was held in Los Angeles,California on 6th of February.

Best pop solo Performance

Easy on me by Adele

Best Pop vocal album

Harry's house by Harry Styles

Best Pop duo/group Performance

Unholy by Sam Smith and Kim Petras

Best Latin Pop album

Pasicros by Ruben Blades and Boca Livre

Most Grammys in Pop Music

Beyonce with 32 Awards.

Juno Awards

Juno awards is held in Canada Annually.This is presented by Canadian Academy of Recording Arts and Sciences.It was held in 14th of March 2023

Billboard Awards

Billboard award ceremony is presented annually by the Billboard Magazine.This year, it is planned to be held on 19th November

Brit Awards

Brit awards is presented by the British Phonography Industry.This was held on 12th of February this year.
